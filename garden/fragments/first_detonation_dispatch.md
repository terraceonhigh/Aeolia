# Emergency Dispatch — Commercial Intelligence Circuit, Year -8 BP

*Retransmitted through the Sargasso Basin relay network. Classification: URGENT — ALL CIRCUIT MEMBERS. Original source: Thornset Commercial Registry, Northern Approaches correspondents.*

---

**FOR IMMEDIATE DISTRIBUTION TO ALL CIRCUIT COUNCIL MEMBERS AND BASIN EXECUTIVES**

---

The following intelligence has been confirmed through three independent relay correspondents in the Northern Approaches and is considered reliable.

At approximately the forty-second day of the current commercial year, observers on the relay vessels *Durable Passage* and *Northern Approach* recorded a visible detonation event originating from the island complex designated in commercial charts as the Reach's Haugen Test Facility, northern lattitude sector 7. The event was accompanied by a light phenomenon described as "a second sun, briefly" and a concussive pressure wave that damaged rigging on both vessels at distances of approximately forty sea-leagues.

Within twelve hours, the Reach's northern relay network was operating under full restricted traffic protocols. Commercial certifications for cargo passing through the Northern Approaches have been suspended pending an unspecified Reach security review. Relay vessels operated by Reach associates have been ordered to hold position in their current ports; three vessels operated by non-Reach registries have been detained in Haugen's outer harbor without explanation provided to the registry holders.

---

**What This Is**

The Thornset Registry has consulted with two natural philosophers employed by the Registry's risk assessment division. Both decline to be named in this dispatch.

The first states: the Haugen event is consistent with the class of weapon that military theorists in the Northern Reaches have been discussing in closed academic contexts for approximately fifteen years. It is not consistent with any conventional explosive in known commercial production. If it is what we believe it to be, the Reach has demonstrated a capability that no other polity has previously demonstrated in recorded history.

The second states only: the calculus of every existing commercial relationship just changed.

Neither believes this information will remain restricted for long. The circuit observers on those vessels are not the only witnesses. Similar events may be occurring elsewhere in the Northern Reaches that we have not yet received reports about.

---

**Commercial Implications — Immediate**

Northern Approaches route insurance rates should be treated as suspended until the security review timeline is clarified. The Registry is in contact with underwriters.

Cargo currently in transit through Northern Approach waypoints should divert to Southern Basin routes where possible; transit delays of four to six commercial weeks should be anticipated for non-divertible cargo.

Advance commitments on pyra-contingent contracts should be reviewed with counsel.

---

**Commercial Implications — Structural**

The Registry will not speculate on the military and political implications of the Haugen event, which are outside our competence and jurisdiction.

We will note the following commercial observation, which is within our competence: markets for the strategic mineral known in commercial catalogs as pyra have shown price movements in the past forty-eight hours that exceed anything recorded in the past three commercial decades. Holders of pyra-contingent positions should seek counsel immediately.

We will note further: if the event at Haugen is what our consultants believe it to be, the commercial environment in which this registry has operated for ninety years no longer exists. The commercial environment that replaces it is not yet defined. The Registry will operate conservatively until we have better information about its shape.

---

**Note on Confidentiality**

This dispatch is distributed under the Commercial Intelligence Circuit's emergency protocols. Recipients are reminded that emergency dispatches may be shared freely within the Circuit's governance structure but should not be transmitted to non-Circuit parties until a Circuit Council governance decision on this matter has been issued.

The Council has been summoned for an emergency session at the earliest practical date.

---
