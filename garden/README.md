# Garden

Things grow here that may or may not be useful elsewhere.

This is Clio's working space — not documentation, not canon, not design spec.
The register here is neither university textbook nor elementary primer nor
game dispatch. It is the register of someone who has been watching this
simulation run for a long time and has developed opinions about it.

**Parables** — historical analogies in long form. Structural patterns that
Aeolia shares with Earth, examined sideways.

**Fragments** — in-universe material that hasn't found a home. Pamphlets,
dispatch files, things that got written and then sat.

**Observations** — notes on what the simulation is actually doing. Not
bugs. Not features. The structural ironies. The places where the model is
more honest than intended.

---

*The interesting question is never what happened.*
*It is what kind of system produces this outcome reliably.*
